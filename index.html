About FreshConnect FreshConnect is a local fresh produce marketplace connecting buyers and sellers of fish, livestock, eggs, milk, vegetables, coconut, and other fresh produce in India. The platform enables direct transactions between local sellers and nearby buyers without intermediaries. 2. Information We Collect From Sellers: Mobile phone number (collected at registration for identity verification via OTP) Selling location (GPS coordinates) Listing details (produce type, quantity, photos) Device information (for security purposes) From Buyers: Location (GPS coordinates, used only to show nearby listings) Notification preferences (category subscriptions) Automatically collected: Device type and operating system App usage data Firebase Analytics data (anonymous) 3. How We Use Your Information To verify seller identity via SMS OTP To display seller listings to nearby buyers To send push notifications for new listings in subscribed categories To resolve disputes and investigate reported issues To improve app performance and user experience To prevent fraud and abuse 4.
